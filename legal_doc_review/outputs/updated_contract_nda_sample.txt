NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is made effective as of February 1, 2024, between Orion Tech Inc. ("Disclosing Party") and Stellar Solutions LLC ("Receiving Party").

1. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION: Confidential Information includes technical data, trade secrets, business plans, software, customer lists, and pricing.

2. OBLIGATIONS: Receiving Party shall (a) keep the Confidential Information strictly confidential, (b) not disclose it to any third party, and (c) not use it for any purpose except evaluating a business opportunity with Disclosing Party.

3. TERM: This Agreement and the confidentiality obligations shall remain in effect for six (6) years from the effective date.

4. EXCEPTIONS: The confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that is (a) publicly available, (b) disclosed by a third party legally, or (c) independently developed.

5. RETURN OF MATERIALS: Upon termination, Receiving Party shall return or destroy all Confidential Information.

6. NO LICENSE: This Agreement does not grant any rights or licenses under any intellectual property.

7. GOVERNING LAW: This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
