To add an indemnity clause to the existing contract, we will insert a new clause after Option 6 and before Limitation of Liability.

**Updated Contract**

CONFIDENTIALITY AGREEMENT

This Confidentiality Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Company A and Company B.

1. Termination: Either party may terminate this Agreement upon 60 days' written notice.
2. Payment Terms: Payment shall be made within 30 days of invoice receipt (Net 30).
3. GDPR Compliance: The parties agree to comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in their handling and processing of personal data, including but not limited to:
* Ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and security of personal data
* Providing individuals with the right to access and rectify their personal data
* Notifying the relevant authorities of any personal data breaches
* Complying with all applicable GDPR requirements for data protection and processing
4. Confidentiality: Parties agree to protect confidential information for 5 years after termination.
5. Indemnity: Each party agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the other from damages, including but not limited to, losses resulting from breach of confidentiality or misappropriation of confidential information.
6. Option: The parties agree that in the event of default by either party under this Agreement, the non-defaulting party shall have the right to terminate this Agreement upon 30 days' written notice.
7. Limitation of Liability: Neither party shall be liable for indirect or consequential damages.

Signed: April 10, 2025.
